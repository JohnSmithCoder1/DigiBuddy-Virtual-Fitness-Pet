# DigiBuddy Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 8, 2026_

DigiBuddy is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with all applicable privacy laws, including the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

DigiBuddy does not require an account or login. DigiBuddy does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information — such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location — and this is true for all users, including children. DigiBuddy does not access your device's email, phone, or webcam features, and does not include social media integrations or advertising.

## HealthKit Data

DigiBuddy integrates with Apple HealthKit to display your progress toward selected fitness goals, including:
- Steps
- Exercise minutes

Access to HealthKit data is entirely optional and can be granted or denied per category within Apple Health settings. **Your health data never leaves your device.** DigiBuddy does not transmit, store, or share HealthKit data with any external servers, third parties, or other apps, and never uses it for advertising, marketing, or data mining. DigiBuddy fully complies with Apple's HealthKit guidelines, ensuring your health data is handled with the highest level of security and privacy.

## Anonymous Usage Analytics

To understand how DigiBuddy is used and to make it better, DigiBuddy collects a small amount of **anonymous** usage information using PostHog, a third-party analytics provider acting as our data processor. This is limited to non-personal events such as completing onboarding, daily check-ins, and app opens.

This analytics data:

- **Never includes your health data.** Your steps, exercise minutes, and fitness goals stay on your device and are never sent to PostHog or anyone else.
- **Contains no personal information.** No name, email, account, contact information, or advertising identifier is collected — from anyone, including children. Events are associated only with a random, app-generated identifier that cannot be traced back to you.
- **Is not used for tracking.** DigiBuddy does not track you across other apps or websites and does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA). Your IP address is discarded and is not stored.

We do not sell your information, and we never use analytics for advertising. You can stop all collection at any time by deleting the app.
