# DigiBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

DigiBuddy does not require an account or login. DigiBuddy does not collect your
name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or advertising
identifier. It does, however, collect a limited amount of pseudonymous app-usage
and technical information, described under **Pseudonymous Usage Analytics** below.

DigiBuddy does not access your device's email, phone, contacts, camera, or
microphone, and does not include advertising or social-media integrations.

## HealthKit Data

DigiBuddy integrates with Apple HealthKit to display your progress toward
selected fitness goals, including:

* Steps
* Exercise minutes

Access to HealthKit data is entirely optional and can be granted or denied per
category within Apple Health settings.

Your health data never leaves your device. DigiBuddy does not transmit, store, or
share your HealthKit step counts, exercise minutes, fitness goals, or raw
progress percentages with PostHog, any other external server, third party, or
other app, and never uses HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or data
mining. DigiBuddy's analytics may record only whether a synchronization feature
is turned on and an already-abstract daily status category (such as a "green
day"); these never contain your underlying HealthKit values or goals. DigiBuddy
follows Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

## Pseudonymous Usage Analytics

To understand how DigiBuddy is used, measure reliability and retention, and
improve the app, DigiBuddy uses PostHog, a third-party analytics provider acting
as our data processor.

Analytics events are associated with a randomly generated, app-created identifier
that links events from the same DigiBuddy installation over time. It is not tied
to an account, name, email address, advertising identifier, or any other directly
identifying information. Because it links events over time, this data is
**pseudonymous** rather than fully anonymous.

The information collected may include:

* App lifecycle events, such as installing, updating, opening, foregrounding, and
  backgrounding the app
* Onboarding progress and whether optional synchronization or notification
  features are enabled
* Derived in-app activity, such as daily status categories, streak counts, Digi
  level, total care days, and time since your previous visit
* Interactions such as in-app coin purchases of virtual items, reminder openings,
  Digi Journey views, and sharing actions
* Basic technical information automatically added by the analytics software, such
  as app version and build, device model and type, operating-system name and
  version, language/region, time zone, and screen dimensions

Analytics never includes:

* Your HealthKit step counts, exercise minutes, fitness-goal values, or raw
  completion percentages
* The name you gave your Digi
* Your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location
* The Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
* Session recordings, screen contents, or automatically captured taps

PostHog processes this information on DigiBuddy's behalf using its United States
cloud service. Reaching PostHog's servers necessarily transmits an IP address,
but DigiBuddy's PostHog configuration is set to discard IP-derived location
information and not retain the IP address.

DigiBuddy does not sell analytics information, use it for advertising, share it
with data brokers, or combine it with information from other companies' apps or
websites.

## No Cross-App Tracking

DigiBuddy does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. It
does not use analytics for targeted advertising or advertising measurement, and
does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA).

"Tracking" here refers to tracking as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency
framework. It does not mean DigiBuddy collects no usage analytics within DigiBuddy
itself.

## Your Choices and Data Retention

DigiBuddy does not currently offer an in-app control for disabling analytics.
Deleting DigiBuddy stops future analytics collection from that installation, but
it does not automatically delete information already transmitted to PostHog.

Analytics information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for the
purposes described above, in accordance with our analytics provider's
data-retention practices, then deleted or aggregated.

To ask about analytics information or request deletion where applicable, contact
us at the address below. Because DigiBuddy collects no account or contact
information, we may need the applicable analytics identifier to locate a
particular record.

## Children's Privacy

DigiBuddy is designed with children's privacy in mind and is intended to align
with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

DigiBuddy does not knowingly collect names, contact information, precise
location, advertising identifiers, or HealthKit values from children. DigiBuddy's
pseudonymous analytics are used only to operate, maintain, analyze, and improve
the app. They are not used to contact children, to serve behavioral advertising,
or to track children across other apps or websites.

Parents or guardians with questions about DigiBuddy's data practices may contact
us using the address below.
